DIVEKICKER

CONTROLS

LEFT ARROW - MOVE LEFT

RIGHT ARROW - MOVE RIGHT

SPACEBAR - JUMP

DOWN ARROW - DIVEKICK TO BOUNCE ON ENEMIES, RESET YOUR JUMPS, AND EARN POINTS

Q - BECOME INVULNERABLE AND SPIN THROUGH ENEMIES TO EARN POINTS

M - TOGGLE MUSIC

N - TOGGLE SOUNDFX

TIPS

YOU ONLY HAVE 2 JUMPS, BUT SUCCESSFUL DIVEKICKS RESET THEM!

Press 'r' to restart!
